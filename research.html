Research Outcomes

My doctoral research developed a comprehensive framework to bridge the gap between technical accessibility and social acceptability.

01. Stigma-Mitigating Design Framework

This framework identifies three core mechanisms of stigma: Visibility Anxiety, Incompetence Shame, and Dependency Labels. It provides a closed-loop methodology for designers to intervene at the technical, social, and psychological levels.

02. The 6 Actionable Guidelines
